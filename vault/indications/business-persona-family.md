# business-persona-family

**Rule.** The business persona packs (`sales`, `seo`, `support`, `business`, `startup-eval`, plus the
extended `marketing`) are **opt-in critique lenses** (no repo-marker auto-detect — `VAULT.md`
`project_type`/`personas.use` only), grounded in tooling that actually exists in the install (sales-*
skills, seo-* agents, PostHog/Bright Data/BOE MCPs, recomputation, grep). Numeric truth routes to the
single shared `business/data-evidence` critic (`_shared/business/`, group-qualified `use_shared` id) —
domain lenses never re-own number checks. Multi-domain projects seat several packs via a `personas.use`
**list**: one architect seat total (primary pack), shared critics load once with **union** overlays,
selection per `_resolution.md` §2.2 (guaranteed domain lens, business cap 4, one-trigger-one-lens,
cross-pack suppression). Dev and business packs never mix in one list.

**Why.** Panel value comes from decorrelated, tool-grounded lenses. Business deliverables are where
confident fabrication is easiest — an ungrounded "critic" just adds correlated opinion. Discovered via
the 2-round panel on the family's own design: cross-pack deference language was dead prose until
multi-pack seating existed; architect+data-evidence+skeptic evicted every domain lens exactly on
high-stakes work; two lenses re-owning one check (SoV method, reflexive discounting) collapses to one
effective vote.

**How to apply.** Adding a business pack: follow `_pack-template.md` + the marketing.md exemplar; bind
only analyzers that exist (else the persona must self-downgrade to advisory); declare decorrelation
boundaries against sibling packs it may co-seat with; route method/arithmetic checks to
`business/data-evidence`; register the pack slug in `_resolution.md` §1/§2.2 and the trigger table.
Evidence rules: no number without a source, graded solid/moderate/thin, vendor stats without primary
source discarded.
